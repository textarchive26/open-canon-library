This library provides free public access to early Christian writings, related canonical traditions, and documented historical timelines.

Different communities preserved different collections of texts throughout history. Some became widely recognized in the West. Others were preserved regionally. Some were lost and rediscovered centuries later.

This project gathers them into one organized space so readers may encounter primary texts directly.
